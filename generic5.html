Coffee Makers Conjoint
Analysis

(SQL,Python)

The main objective of this project is to help Philips Coffee Makers understand the importance of product attributes to consumers by conjoint analysis. We created a mixture multinomial logit models to figured out the best product attributes combination, and evaluated the market potential and impact on other existing products in the marketplace.
