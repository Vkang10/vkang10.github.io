TROPICANA PRICE
STRATEGY

(SAS, Excel)

In order to determine the optimal retail price and maximize the gross profit, we created linear regression, log-log and semi-log models using SAS to discover associations between features and sales. By calculating the expected gross profit in Excel and based on the Mantissa Pricing Strategy, especially the effect of pricing ending in 9 on consumer purchasing, we recommended $3.19 as the retail price.
