TROPICANA PRICE
STRATEGY

(SAS, Excel)

summary

To standardize the price of Tropicana 64 oz. and to maximize its gross profit, we analyze sales data to optimize the retail price by figuring out the effects of price, deals, and other relevant variables on sales.

The data used for analysis is from recording sales of orange juice category: Tropicana 64 ounces from grocery retailer Nickâ€™s. To recognize the best retail price for Tropicana 64 oz, we run three models: linear regression, log-log, and semi-log in SAS. By comparing Q-Q plots, histograms and adjusted R-square, we finally choose the semi-log model and generate statistical explanations for key drivers. By calculating the expected gross profit in Excel and based on the Mantissa Pricing Strategy, especially the effect of pricing ending in 9 on consumer purchasing, we recommended $3.19 as the retail price with an estimated 32% increase in total profit
