Customer Lifetime
Value Predicting

(SQL,Python)

This project aims to select the best model for Google Merchandise Store to predict the pCLV of their newly acquired customers within 90 days after being acquired. We extracted and transform data collected from the Google Merchandise Store with SQL and conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) in Python to understand data distribution and drop outliers.

By training 4 classification model to classify return customers and 5 regression models to predict future value for repeat purchasers only, we finally improved combined model accuracy (MAE: 18.76), realizing a 40% improvement from the baseline model. And through this analysis, we identify important factors that are linked to higher future revenue, which could help google merchandise and other google clients target the right customers and develop a smart bidding strategy on paid search promotions.
